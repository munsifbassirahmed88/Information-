GAMES:

Games are structured forms of play that entertain, educate, and sometimes challenge players both mentally and physically. They are an essential part of human culture and development, offering opportunities for creativity, learning, and social interaction.

Categories of Games

  • Video Games:

    These are digital or electronic games played on computers, consoles, or mobile devices. They include genres like action, adventure, role-playing (RPG), simulation, and strategy games.

  • Board Games:

    Played on a flat surface, board games like chess, Monopoly, and Scrabble involve strategy, chance, or a mix of both.

  • Card Games:

    Games like Poker, Uno, and Solitaire rely on decks of cards and often involve strategic thinking or luck.

  • Sports and Physical Games:

    Activities such as soccer, basketball, or tag involve physical effort and teamwork.

  • Puzzle Games:

    These games, such as crosswords, Sudoku, or jigsaw puzzles, focus on problem-solving and critical thinking.
